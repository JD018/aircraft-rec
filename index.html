Program Synopsis

This program will search the web for aircraft images and generate a quiz similar to the Aircraft Rec ground competition event. Ten questions will be generated and you will be given three choices of aircraft to choose from. If the image is not relevant to the aircraft, you may click the Regenerate button to display an alternate image.
